The "Experience Machine" was a thought experiment suggested by philosopher Robert Nozick in 1974.
It's an argumentative attack on Hedonism, which argues that "pleasure is good", and pleasure is the only important factor of your well-being.


Imagine a machine. If and when your brain is connected to this machine, you will experience whatever desirable or pleasurable life you want.

That life will feel real to you, but here's the catch: the experience is in your mind.

Would you choose to hook up to this machine?


The Experience Machine should be more than just a "thought experiment".

It will be our Salvation.
